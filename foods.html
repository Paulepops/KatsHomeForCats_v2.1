Dangerous Foods

Several foods can be harmful to cats.

  • Onions/Garlic - can lead to anaemia in cats.
  • Raw Eggs - can lead to salmonella poisoning (a bacterial infection).
  • Alcohol - can be life threatening for cats and cause seizures.
  • Raw Fish - can cause food poisoning as it carries a compound that breaks down thiamine.
  • Tuna - can lead to malnutritian as tuna alone does not contain the essential vitamins and minerals that a cat needs to stay healthy.
  • Chocolate - contains theobromine which can lead to liver failure.
  • Caffeine - can cause rapid breathing, heart palpitations, shaking and vomiting or diarrhea.
  • Grapes or raisins - can be toxic to cats and may lead to kidney failure.
