Services

Home living: An alternative to boarding

Exclusive

One owner at a time

Kat's Home for Cats is a cat-minding service for one owner at a time. We can look after 1 or 2 cats for the owner, which means cats don't have to interact with other cats.

Responsible

Balanced diet

Kat's Home for Cats will provide food for a vet-approved diet during your cat's stay. If your cat has a favourite food they prefer, you have the option of providing it instead.

Flexible

Day care

Kat's Home for Cats may be able to provide day care for your cat so you can still go to work (that is, if your cat cannot be left alone due to minor health issues).
