Cats I've loved ...

Blessed are those who love cats, for they shall never be lonely

Muffy

Muffy was in care for about six weeks. She was a very timid cat who suffered anxiety but responded well to love and affection.

Felix

This is Felix. Felix was cared for over two months. Felix didn't climb much but he rubbed up against every sharp corner he could find. He was a confident cat.

Billie

Billie was in care for two months. She loved investigating the house and could sit for hours watching the birds through the window.

Andre

This is Andre. He was the alpha-male in the family and Moe's brother. Moe deferred to him in all things, but they loved curling up together. He was a beautiful, loving cat.

Moe

This is Moe. Moe was Andre's brother and always deferred to Andre when they were both together. He loved jumping into suitcases when we were packing to go away or just while we had it open!

Geelong
