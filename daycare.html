Day Care

Kat's Home for Cats may be able to provide day care for your cat so you can still go to work (that is, if your cat cannot be left alone due to minor health issues).

If your cat has a temporary need of closer monitoring during the day, but you're not going away at all and can have them at home when you are, Kat's Home for Cats may be able to help.

You will need to discuss your requirements with us to see if your cat's needs can be met by Kat's Home for Cats.
