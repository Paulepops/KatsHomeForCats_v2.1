Balanced diet

Kat's Home for Cats will provide your cat(s) with a vet-approved diet during their stay. We use Hill's Science Diet. You can read more about this diet on the Hills website.

There isn't a single diet that is ideal for all cats, but in general, most cats thrive when they eat foods that are high in moisture and protein, and low in carbohydrates.

If your cat has a favourite food they prefer, or if you know your cat won't like the Hill's Science Diet, you have the option to provide their food instead. Kat's Home for Cats offers a small discount on the daily cost of care if you elect to provide the food for your cat(s).
