Lice

Lice can be seen as white or brown grain like spots (immature adult eggs) attached to your cat's fur. Make sure your cat is living in sanitary conditions, and their environment is cleaned regularly.

Your cat needs to have its own areas for feeding and its litter tray. Overcrowding of cats can cause the spread of lice so keeping your cat groomed regularly is also very important.
